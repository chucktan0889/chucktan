WHAT IS PLAYCON

PLAYCON is a gaming community and creative collective formed from the love of playing video games with friends. What started as a community with the goal of creating content about video games has evolved and grown into facebook gaming platform. PLAYCON is created by Chuck Fernandez in the Philippines, and regularly updates games, manages pages, and creates content that anyone who loves gaming can enjoy.
PLAYCON is about being a community that enjoys having fun together and creating a positive social impact through gaming. We continuously aim to be a good influence in the overall gaming community, with a battlecry that urges our community to PLAY AND LEARN!

WHO IS PLAYCON
